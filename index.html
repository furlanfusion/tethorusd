How Tethor USD Works

A simple, transparent process ensures the stability and reliability of TUSD.

1

Deposit USD

Users deposit US dollars into Tethor's regulated custodial accounts.

3

Redeem Anytime

Holders can redeem TUSD for USD at any time 1:1.

2

Mint TUSD

An equivalent amount of TUSD is minted and delivered to the user's wallet.

4

Burn TUSD

Redeemed TUSD is burned and equivalent USD is returned to the user.
